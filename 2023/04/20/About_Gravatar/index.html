About Gravatar

Posted on 2023-04-20 In Learn Views: Valine:

When I was trying to add a comment function in my blog website, I realized that my previous profile picture was lacking in aesthetics. Such as this one:

Or may be this one:

So, to make you guys enjoy sharing your ideas to me, I have decided to find a way for you to be able to choose your own avatar.

Then, Gravatar comes to me.

I really like its design. Gravatar powers your public profile, visible wherever you post, comment, and interact online. That’s awecome, but the more excellent point is that it says “Gravatar is free for everyone—forever.”

I suggest that everyone should try it. All you have to do is to Gravatar Website, fill in your username and email, and then choose a favorite photo to upload. This will create a unique avatar that can be used in various comment sections. Just make sure to include your email information when commenting.

Anyway, this comment function is useful. Crave your thoughts!
